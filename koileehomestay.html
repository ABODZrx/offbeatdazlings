How To Reach

To reach Koilee Homestay at Tukvar Tea Estate from Darjeeling market, you can follow these directions:

1. The homestay is located in the Tukvar Tea Estate, which is about 13 km away from Darjeeling market. You can hire a taxi or take a shared jeep from Darjeeling taxi stand to reach Tukvar Tea Estate.

2. Once you reach Tukvar Tea Estate, follow the signs that lead to the homestay. The homestay is located about 2 km away from the main road, so you will need to walk or hire a local taxi to reach the homestay.

3. If you choose to walk, follow the signs that lead to Koilee Homestay. The walk will take you through the lush green tea gardens of Tukvar Tea Estate, offering stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

4. If you choose to hire a taxi, ask the driver to take you to Koilee Homestay at Tukvar Tea Estate. The homestay is located on the road that connects Darjeeling to Mirik, so it's easy to find.

Overall, reaching Koilee Homestay at Tukvar Tea Estate from Darjeeling market is a scenic journey that takes you through the picturesque tea gardens of Tukvar Tea Estate. Whether you choose to walk or take a taxi, the journey is definitely worth the effort.
