Nestled in the lush green hills of Darjeeling, Chatakpur is a hidden gem that beckons adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. As you wind your way up the winding mountain roads, the dense forest and misty air envelop you in a sense of tranquility and awe. As you reach the top, you're greeted by breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. The misty haze creates an ethereal atmosphere, as if you've stepped into a fairy tale world. The chirping of birds and the rustling of leaves add to the serene ambiance, making it the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The true beauty of Chatakpur lies in its unspoiled natural beauty. The forest is teeming with a variety of flora and fauna, including rare birds like the colorful Himalayan monal and the elusive red panda. The winding hiking trails take you through the heart of the forest, where you can spot vibrant butterflies, wildflowers, and towering trees. As night falls, the twinkling stars light up the sky, and the cool mountain breeze carries the sounds of distant wildlife. You can cozy up by a warm campfire, savoring a hot cup of tea, and lose yourself in the magic of the surroundings. Chatakpur is a place that will leave an indelible mark on your soul. It's not just a destination, but an experience that will stay with you long after you leave.

How To Reach

To reach Chatakpur, a serene village in Darjeeling, you can follow these directions:

1. Chatakpur is located about 20 km away from Darjeeling town. You can hire a taxi or take a shared jeep from Darjeeling taxi stand to reach Chatakpur.

2. The journey takes about an hour and a half to two hours, depending on the road conditions and traffic. The road to Chatakpur is narrow and winding, with steep inclines and sharp turns, so it's advisable to hire an experienced driver.

3. As you near Chatakpur, you will reach a checkpoint where you need to show your identity proof and pay a small entry fee. The fee goes towards the maintenance of the village and its surrounding forest.

4. Once you reach Chatakpur, you can explore the village on foot. The village is small and walkable, and the hiking trails that lead to the nearby forest and hilltops start right from the village.

Overall, reaching Chatakpur requires a bit of effort, but the journey is definitely worth it for the stunning views and peaceful ambiance that await you in this hidden gem of Darjeeling.


Alternative Way To Reach Chatakpur

Chatakpur by cycling is a great way to explore the beautiful hills of Darjeeling and enjoy the scenic views along the way. Here's how you can reach Chatakpur by cycling:

1. Start from Darjeeling town and follow the road towards Jorebungalow. The road is uphill and can be challenging, so make sure you have a good quality bike and appropriate gear.

2. As you near Jorebungalow, take the right turn towards Sukhia Pokhri. This road is relatively flat and offers some beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

3. Follow the road towards Sukhia Pokhri and continue on towards Manebhanjang. The road here is steep and winding, and you will need to take breaks along the way to catch your breath.

4. From Manebhanjang, take the road towards Chatakpur. This road is a mix of flat and uphill stretches, with some sections that are quite steep.

5. As you near Chatakpur, the road becomes a narrow trail that leads through the forest. This section can be challenging for cycling, so be prepared to push your bike for short stretches if needed.

6. Once you reach Chatakpur, you can explore the village and nearby hiking trails on foot. You can also rest and rejuvenate at one of the homestays or tea houses in the village.

Overall, cycling to Chatakpur requires a good level of fitness and endurance, but it's a rewarding experience that allows you to enjoy the natural beauty of Darjeeling at a slower pace. Remember to carry enough water, snacks, and sunscreen, and always follow traffic rules and safety guidelines while cycling.
